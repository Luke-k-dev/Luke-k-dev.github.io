My Applications

snd.wave

snd.wave analyzes your music library and presents you with stats about how you listen to music.

snd.wave features:

  • Stats about EVERYTHING.
  • Top Lists: see you favorite artists, songs, albums and genres.
  • Monthly and Yearly Stats. (Stat recording begins when you install snd.wave)
  • Playlist analytics: see tons of stats and play your playlists in snd.wave!
  • Leaderboards: see how your music stats stack up against your friends and the world.
  • Cleaner: Sometimes you download a song or two that are just terrible. snd.wave can help! Let snd.wave find those songs so you can delete them.
  • Charts and Graphs: See your music stats visualized.
  • Stat Player: a music player that also displays stats for the now playing song.
  • Swipe to Play: swipe any song to start playback.
  • Dark Mode for easy viewing at night!
  • Your Music Quick View: a lock screen widget that displays stats about currently
    playing music.

Tempo Audio

Tempo is the first adaptive audio app for iOS that adjusts your music volume based on what you are doing so you don’t have to.

Walking with music? Let Tempo turn it down so you can talk to your friend. Slowing down at a stop sign? Tempo will turn down the music so you can listen for cars. Biking quickly? Tempo can turn up your music so you can hear it over the wind. On a ski lift? No worries, Tempo will start the music again once you get off. Tempo features:

  • Editable speed and volume curves.
  • Multiple modes and the option to create more.
  • Ski Mode: Tempo understands how ski lifts work and handles audio accordingly.
  • A WatchOS app: Start/Stop Tempo and change modes on the go.
  • Easy Off: Receive a push notification on your lock screen when using tempo to easily disable it.
  • Support for both MPH and KPH
  • Tempo will automatically disable itself whenever the volume is manually adjusted.

Mix.It

Work with AI and advanced filters to create the perfect playlist for any occasion.

Mix.It offers two ways to build mixes.

Regular Mixes: Regular Mixes will ask for a couple of hints to get started and then will use those hints along with AI and your music listening history to recommend songs. These recommendations are placed on to cards. Swipe right to add it to the playlist, swipe left to skip it!

Smart Mixes: Smart Mixes work like Smart Playlists in iTunes. Simply add filters to the Smart Mix and Mix.It will automatically add all of the songs in your library that satisfy the filters.

Mix.It features a beautiful music player, capable of adapting its theme to the currently playing song’s artwork. The Mix.It Player can also recommend songs to play next and supports “Play Next” and “Play Later.”
